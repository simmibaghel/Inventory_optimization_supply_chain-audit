Inefficient inventory management leads to high carrying costs and lost sales due to stockouts. This project applied data-driven optimization techniques to streamline supply chain operations, resulting in a **12% reduction in annual carrying costs** and achieving a **97% on-time distribution rate**.

## üéØ Key Objectives
* **Safety Stock Optimization:** Developed a dynamic model to calculate inventory buffers based on demand volatility and supplier lead times.

* **Cost Reduction:** Minimized "Dead Stock" by identifying low-turnover items through ABC Analysis (Categorizing inventory by value/importance).

## üìä Methodology
1.  **Demand Profiling:** Used SQL to segment products into A, B, and C categories based on their contribution to total revenue.

3.  **Fulfillment RCA:** Performed a deep-dive analysis into shipping data, discovering that 60% of delays occurred at a specific transit hub.
